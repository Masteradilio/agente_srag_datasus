About the Opportunity
Indicium AI is seeking a Senior AI Engineer to lead the development and implementation of complex, large-scale AI solutions, defining the technical direction, and mentoring other engineers. You will be responsible for the architecture, design, and optimization of AI systems, ensuring high performance, scalability, and reliability.

 

Key Responsibilities

Lead the design, development, and implementation of cutting-edge AI solutions utilizing LLMs, prompt engineering, RAG, agent development, and fine-tuning.
Define the architecture and design of scalable, robust, and efficient AI systems.
Optimize AI and data pipelines for maximum performance and cost-effectiveness.
Lead the integration of AI solutions with complex and mission-critical systems.
Establish and maintain best practices for AI/ML development, testing, and deployment (MLOps and/or LLMOps).
Mentor and guide junior and mid-level AI engineers.
Conduct research and evaluate new technologies and approaches in AI.
Contribute to the definition of the company's AI strategy.
Resolve complex and critical problems related to AI systems.
